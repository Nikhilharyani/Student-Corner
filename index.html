Student Corner

The one stop shop for remuse building, registering any complaints or an easy way to attend online classes. We have it all.

We have build this application as an essential problem solving tool for all the students of NITJ, keeping in mind the day to day academic hinderances the students are facing in this world of online education. We have tried to reduce the number of steps and provide a direct approach to things. We as a team have focused on three different issues: an official NITJ resume for all the students, a quick access to all the online classes and an online complaintbox. The need for resume can be felt by both the interviewer and the students. The number of steps it takes me to open my laptop and get into the respective class will really amaze you,thus bringing in the OneClass App - one click away to any of your class. The last but not the least, the idea is credited to our mentor, Dr. Dilbag Singh to create a virtual complaintbox where one can register and view all the complaints.
